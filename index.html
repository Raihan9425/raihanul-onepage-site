About Me

MD. Raihanul Islam is an incoming PhD student in the Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences at the University of California, Riverside. He earned his BSc in Civil & Water Resources Engineering from the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST), Bangladesh, and spent almost six years at the Institute of Water Modelling honing his skills in geomorphological analysis, hydrodynamic modelling, and GIS-based assessments. His research focuses on river and delta morphodynamics.

Research Interests

  • River Morphology
  • GIS & Remote Sensing
  • Hydrodynamic Modelling
